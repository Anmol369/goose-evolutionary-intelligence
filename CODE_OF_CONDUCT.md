# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) version 2.1 as our Code of Conduct.

## Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. Please review the full Contributor Covenant 2.1 at: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
